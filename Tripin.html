Objective

Our team want to understand the thought process travelers go through to finalized a destination and their preferences of accommodation.

The Approach

In order to get the fastest research responses in a limited time, we conducted 1-to1 interviews, not only it's a straighforward approached to collect first hand data, also an opportunity to interact with users to dig deep of their thoughts process and pain for future development

Problems

What I’ve learned from our interviews was that people wants to go out and interact with each other, what also in their minds were not only the choice of city to visit, but the overall safety of travelling.
The purpose of our product is to deliver a solution to help users to better understand the current health situation and make planning a trip less stressful.
